Confirm and finish purchase


In this screen you can see summary of your order. If you wish to change the values of previous fields or to cancel the whole process, you can click on "Back" or "Cancel", respectively. If you want to confirm the purchase of the selected products, click "Purchase". Be aware that after pressing this button the order will be completed and you are not going to be able to revert the purchase.



After confirming the purchase, a file with name "ID_DateOfPurchase.txt" will be created and saved in your computer. You are also going to be brought again to the starting screen, from where you can place a new order if you want.
