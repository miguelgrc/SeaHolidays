Personal information and payment methods


In order to go on with the purchase, you will have to introduce the requested data. This data is: name, surnames, ID card, e-mail, payment method (where you have to specify the method and then introduce the information related to the selected method), phone number and address. Take into account that the format for each field must be correct. If you fail to introduce some data, a warning will be shown telling you what you have to correct. Some fields include hints in order to clarify the format for the given data.



Note that the total price of the order is also shown in this window. Remember that you can always go back pressing "Back" to change the information introduced in the previous windows, or "Cancel" in order to abort the process and start again in the main window.

Once you have introduced correct information, you can follow to the next step pressing "Next". Now a bill will be generated and you will need to confirm the purchase.
